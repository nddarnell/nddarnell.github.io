Mac Miller Fan Page

Bio

Malcolm McCormick, professionally known as Mac Miller was born in 1992 in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. Mac Miller was an American Rapper and Record Producer, he began his musical career in the hip hop scene at the age of 15, three years later he signed with Pittsburgh independent record label Rostrum Records. Over the following 2 years Mac Miller released two mixtapes "KIDS" and "Best Day Ever" which launched him into stardom. Miller's debut studio album, Blue Slide Park (2011), became the first independently distributed album to top the US Billboard 200 since 1995. In 2014 Miller left Rostrum Records for Warner Bros. Records, he released an additional 3 albums before his unfortunate death in 2018 due to substance abuse. Over a year after Mac Millers death his album "Circles" was released in 2020 recording a total of 6 studio albums along with many mixtapes.
